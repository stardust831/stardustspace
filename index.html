Why Stardust?

"All elements that make up the universe, the earth and the human body are made from stars and their life cycles. We are all made of stardust."

This anonymous quote inspired me in highschool, and I've been using the nickname Stardust since. It was also one of the things that inspired me to study physics, though I no longer follow that path.

However, the night sky displaying stars and galaxys still inspire me, and to this day I think they show a part of who I am.

The Stardust Space is a space for me, Stardust. It is also a space for stardust, in the literal sense, and can also mean the outer space filled with stardust.

About Me

I am 23 years old, and as an undergraduate I studied physics. I am a student at Seoul National University. However, during my undergraduate years I lost interest in physics, and am now interested in STS, or Science and Technology Studies. I am considering studying this field as a graduate student, but for now I am taking a break from school and studying programming as a hobby.

I am queer! I identify as pansexual nonbinary, and my pronouns are she/they. I also am in a nonmonogamy relationship and I practice BDSM.
